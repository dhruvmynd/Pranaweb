Data Deletion Instructions

You can delete your Vayu account and all associated data in two ways:

Option 1: Delete through your account settings

  1. Sign in to your Vayu account
  2. Go to Profile Settings
  3. Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account"
  4. Confirm deletion by entering your email address

Option 2: Contact Support

Email privacy@vayu-prana.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request" and include:

  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • Request to delete your account and data

Note: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. This will remove:

  • Your profile information
  • Practice history and statistics
  • Mood tracking data
  • All other associated data

For more information about how we handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy.
